London is the capital of England in the United Kingdom Located in Europe. It is a land a deep history and a large variety of attractions. One of the most recognizable landmarks in London is Elizabeth Tower (Big Ben) which is a British icon. London is well known for its deep & rich history, free museums, teathers, and the monarchy.

Believe it or not London doesn't work within a true monarchy but within a constitutional monarchy where the monarch act as head of state and is limited by a consitution, day to day responsabilities of the government are ran by elected members of parliment.

London is remarked for being increadibly tourist friendly due to the transportation in the form of the Tube and maps. Another great reason tourists come to London is due to it acting as a traveling hub of sorts with trains and flights going to many major cities in Europe.
